About Subarna

I am a computer vision researcher at Intel AI since May 2018. I received my PhD in Electrical and Computer Enginnering from University of California San Diego. My PhD advisors were Professor Truong Nguyen and Professor Serge Belongie on semantic video segmentation. I am an alumna of Video processing group at UC San Diego and SE(3) computer vision group at Cornell Tech. My research interests include computer vison and video processing. Previously, I worked in STMicroelectronics in its Advanced System Technology (AST) group for over 6 years on computer vision and video processing in general. Prior to that she worked in Interra Systems on video analyzer.

I earned my MS Research from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi and a Bachelor of Technology (Computer Science and Engineering) from Kalyani Govt. Enginnering College. I am from Kalyani, a beautiful town in West Bengal, India.
