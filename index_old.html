About Subarna

Subarna Tripathi is a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Enginnering at UCSD. Her research interests include computer vison and video processing. She is currently working with Professor Truong Nguyen and Professor Serge Belongie on object segmentation and detection. Previously, she worked in STMicroelectronics in its Advanced System Technology (AST) group for over six years on computer vision and video processing in general. Prior to that she worked in Interra Systems on video analyzer.

She earned an MS (Electrical Enginnering) from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi and a Bachelor of Technology (BTech in Computer Science and Engineering) from Kalyani Govt. Enginnering College. She is from West Bengal, India.

She likes to listen to Hindustani classical music. One of her recent favourites is the rendition by Kaushiki Chakraborty. She enjoys singing as well. Some of her casual performances are here.
